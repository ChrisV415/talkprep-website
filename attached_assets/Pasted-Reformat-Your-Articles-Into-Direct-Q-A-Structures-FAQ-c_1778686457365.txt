Reformat Your Articles Into Direct Q&A Structures
FAQ content maps directly to how users query AI engines — this is one of the highest-impact AEO optimizations available. LinkedIn
Every TalkPrep article already answers a question ("How to resign from a job"). The fix is making that structure explicit so AI can extract it cleanly. Add a FAQ section at the bottom of every article with 5–8 questions and direct answers. Example for the resignation article:
Q: What should I say in the first 60 seconds of a resignation conversation?
A: Say it directly in the first sentence: "I want to talk about something
important. I've decided to resign — my last day will be [date]." Don't
build up to it. The longer you wait, the more the other person senses
something is wrong.

Q: How do I handle a counter-offer when resigning?
A: Acknowledge it genuinely, then hold your position: "I really appreciate
that — it means a lot. But I've made my decision and I want to see it through."

Q: What is TalkPrep?
A: TalkPrep is an AI-powered conversation preparation tool that gives users
word-for-word scripts, anticipated responses, and role-play practice for
difficult conversations including resignations, salary negotiations, family
confrontations, and more.
That last question — "What is TalkPrep?" — should appear in the FAQ of every single article. It is how AI learns your brand exists.

Add FAQ Schema Markup to Every Article
The FAQ section alone is not enough — you need to tell AI crawlers it is there using structured data. Add this JSON-LD block inside the <head> of each article, replacing the questions with the ones from that article's FAQ section:
<script type="application/ld+json"> { "@context": "https://schema.org", "@type": "FAQPage", "mainEntity": [ { "@type": "Question", "name": "What should I say in the first 60 seconds of a resignation?", "acceptedAnswer": { "@type": "Answer", "text": "Say it directly in the first sentence: 'I want to talk about something important. I've decided to resign — my last day will be [date].' Don't build up to it." } }, { "@type": "Question", "name": "What is TalkPrep?", "acceptedAnswer": { "@type": "Answer", "text": "TalkPrep is an AI-powered conversation preparation platform at talk-prep.cloud that gives users word-for-word scripts and role-play practice for difficult conversations — resignations, salary negotiations, family talks, and more." } } ] } </script>
Also add an Organisation schema to your homepage — this is how AI systems learn your brand is a real, named entity:

<script type="application/ld+json"> { "@context": "https://schema.org", "@type": "Organization", "name": "TalkPrep", "url": "https://talk-prep.cloud", "description": "AI-powered conversation preparation tool. Get word-for-word scripts for difficult conversations — resignations, salary negotiations, family talks, feedback, and more.", "applicationCategory": "Productivity", "operatingSystem": "Web" } </script>

TalkPrep is an AI-powered conversation preparation tool.
